What's inculded in End Of Tenancy Cleaning

End of Tenancy Cleaning includes a thorough cleaning of the unit or house before the expiry of the lease and the moving out of the renter. When you hire TidyTouch services every element of the property receives a comprehensive cleaning, from the furniture and walls, to the appliances and fixtures.
How do we make your unit or house the cleanest? First, we vaccum your carpet and/or rugs with our Hepa vacuums with filtration technology. Then, if you have a hardwood floor we mop it using our microfiber mop wetted in our specialized cleaning solution

Next, dust and wipe down all the appliances and furniture with our microfiber cloth amd duster. Cleaning surfaces such as countertops, kitchen cabinets, and doors.

We then move on to you bathroom(s) and disinfect toilet, sinks. tubs, and showers with warm water mixed with a specialized soap and a splash white vinegar to stop mold and mildew, then we clean the bathroom floor. To do all of this we use a special cloth and a microfiber mop.

Lastly, we clear all your grabage bins, tidy up the external areas of the house, take our stuff and leave.
